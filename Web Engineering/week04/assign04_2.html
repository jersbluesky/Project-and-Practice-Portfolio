About Us

The Creation of the Kimball Collective

In 2022, a collection of professional composers, conductors, performers, and music educators with years of collective experience bringing crafting both music and the musician came together to form Kimball Creative Music Transcriptions. Our goal is to form a conduit of communication between those in education and professional music production. By doing this, we can not only provide the service of transcribing custom music, but create an industry standard that will enable musicians at every level to connect with the music they are bringing to the world.
