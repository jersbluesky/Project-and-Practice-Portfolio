About Me

WHO AM I?:
My name is Jeremy Johnson. I go by Jer.

WHAT AM I DOING HERE?:

WHERE AM I FROM?:
I grew up in Boise, Idaho, though I'm originally from Salt Lake City, Utah.

A Short Biography

I was born in Boise, Idaho, to Mom and Dad, and enjoyed the company of siblings. My dog's name was Bear. He was a good dog, and my family is a good family. We all still keep in touch, except for Bear, who is likely dead.

I went on a mission to Venezuela, then had to leave Venezuela in secret, wound up in Chile. For further details, look for a book I probably will never write about it. It was a real adventure, and I really should write a book.

After my mission, I completed a degree in Music Education, and became a band director. I got married to a pretty lady, and had cute children. I enjoy them all quite a bit.

Though I loved teaching music, I found myself rarely having time for my family. After prayer and fasting, I made the difficult decision to change careers. I reached out to my Bishop, who mentioned the BYU Pathways program. I initially didn't think I'd qualify, having gotten a degree already. It turns out, God really does want me in this new career, as there is an exception to that rule for online only students, so I started down the path.

Here I am, now, back in Boise. I taught in Oregon and Utah, and finally circled back to where I started. I work for a healthcare company called Primary Health Medical Group as an IT Technician, and I can't wait to graduate and work my way towards upper-level tech jobs.

That's who I am. Who are you?
